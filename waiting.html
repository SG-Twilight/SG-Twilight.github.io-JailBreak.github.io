Waiting Until The Coast Is Clear

Since you're such a patient person, you made the choice to just wait in the woods until this whole situation blows over and the cops forget you tried to commit a felony. You fall asleep against a tree as the sun sets and have peaceful dreams.

You wake a bit later to see a figure standing in front of you. Your vision clears and you realize it's a cop, and not just one, but a whole SWAT Team.

"We got em' boys," one of the cops says into a walkie talkie.

You ask in shock, still sitting against the tree, "How did you guys find me?!"

The leader of the SWAT Team replies, "Dude, you literally just ran across the street into the bushes. We could see you the entire time."
